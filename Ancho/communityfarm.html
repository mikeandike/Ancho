ANCHORED BY AGRICULTURE

Fanita Ranch embraces its connection to the land, with a working farm, vineyards and orchards to provide fresh produce for the community.

PERMACULTURE FARM SETS THE TONE FOR THE COMMUNITY

The centrally located farm at Fanita Ranch will help establish the community’s sense of place and connection to the land.

The professionally managed 30-acre organic farm will use permaculture techniques that mimic natural ecosystems, minimizing the use of energy and water and maximizing recycling.

The high quality, local produce will be available to residents and local restaurants. Community events, classes and activities may be developed to enhance the community’s agrarian sensibility.

Acres of working vineyards and orchards will greet you at both entrances to the neighborhood

Vineyards and orchards will be professionally farmed, providing quality produce to all of Santee
