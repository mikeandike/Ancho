SUSTAINABLE HOMES AND NEIGHBORHOODS

A range of home types and styles fit the needs of a growing community, from young families to empty nesters and everyone in between.

FANITA RANCH OFFERS A DIVERSE VARIETY OF HOMES TO MEET SANTEE’S NEEDS

Santee needs homes designed for its next generation of young people, as well as empty nesters looking to downsize, executives seeking to locate businesses in Santee, and families who are attracted to Santee’s small town character.

Our plan breaks out into three distinct neighborhoods, Fanita Commons, Orchard Village and Vineyard Village, each carefully planned with a focus on agriculture.

FANITA COMMONS & ORCHARD VILLAGE

Fanita Commons & Orchard Village feature a centrally located working farm helping establish the community’s sense of place and connection to the land. A large belt of open space ties the community together.

VINEYARD VILLAGE

Vineyard Village is a neighborhood for all stages of life, featuring dedicated parks and open space, connected with trails. Smaller homes and shops create a quaint village center, while larger single-family homes complete the village.
